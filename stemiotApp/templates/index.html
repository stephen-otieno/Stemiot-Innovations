About

Stemiot Softwares is a forward-thinking tech company driven by a passion for innovation, problem-solving, and digital transformation. We specialize in building intelligent, user-centered software and web solutions that address real-world challenges across industries. From intuitive mobile and web applications to secure database systems and automation tools, our mission is to empower businesses, institutions, and communities through technology. At Stemiot, we believe that great software should not only function — it should inspire, simplify, and create lasting impact.



Stemiot Softwares Project Showcase

Our diverse portfolio showcases the creativity, collaboration, and technical expertise that drive our mission forward. Here are some of the innovative projects we’ve delivered so far.

About us

GenderEquity

A platform to raise awareness and fight against gender-based violence by providing reporting tools, educational content, and support services.

Swiftcare

A hospital management system aiming to streamline operations, including patient registration, appointment scheduling, and medical record management, enhancing service delivery in healthcare settings.

TransJustice

TransJustice is a digital platform designed to enhance transparency and access to justice for victims within police stations. It aims to digitize the Occurrence Book (OB), enabling efficient reporting, case tracking, and accountability.

FiloArts

An innovative platform for visual artists to display and sell their artwork online, connecting creatives with potential buyers and art enthusiasts.

Services

Application Development

Stemiot Softwares specializes in developing robust and scalable applications tailored for both desktop and mobile platforms. We focus on functionality, user experience, and performance to deliver solutions that meet specific business or personal needs.

Web Development

Our web development services cover the design and creation of interactive, responsive, and visually appealing websites. We build dynamic web solutions using modern technologies to ensure seamless access across devices and browsers.

Database Management

Stemiot Softwares offers comprehensive database services, including the design, creation, and maintenance of secure and efficient databases. We ensure structured data storage, optimized performance, and reliable backup systems to support your application's functionality and business intelligence needs.

Cybercafé Services

Stemiot Softwares also offers essential cybercafé services including printing, photocopying, scanning, document editing, online form assistance, branding, and general IT support. We aim to provide fast, affordable, and reliable digital services to support both individual and business needs.
